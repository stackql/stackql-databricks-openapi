DELETE
/api/2.1/accounts/{account_id}/budgets/{budget_id}
Deletes a budget configuration for an account. Both account and budget configuration are specified by ID. This cannot be undone.
Path parameters
code: account_id
required
uuid
Databricks account ID of any type. For non-E2 account types, get your account ID from the
Accounts Console
code: budget_id
required
uuid
The Databricks budget configuration ID.

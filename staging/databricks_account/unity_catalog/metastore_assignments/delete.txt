DELETE
/api/2.0/accounts/{account_id}/workspaces/{workspace_id}/metastores/{metastore_id}
Deletes a metastore assignment to a workspace, leaving the workspace with no metastore.
Path parameters
code: account_id
required
uuid
Databricks account ID of any type. For non-E2 account types, get your account ID from the
Accounts Console
.
code: workspace_id
required
int64
Workspace ID.
code: metastore_id
required
uuid
Unity Catalog metastore ID
Responses
200
The metastore assignment was successfully deleted.
The metastore assignment was successfully deleted.
